    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is Maths Daily free to use?

    Yes, Maths Daily is 100% free and will always be free to use for anyone, anytime, anywhere. While accounts are not needed, they are recommend for frequent visitors to the site. We collect cookies to personalise your experience, and cache you name/email to enhance your experience.

    I've forgotten my password, and/or been locked out of my account. What can I do?

    Don't worry, this is a common occurrence

    To get you back into your account, we will need to prove your identity. Normally, we will send you a verification email to your account's email address, and then you will be able to login again with a new password.

    I have a suggestion for this site

    All feedback is welcome, and we will try to respond to all suggestions as soon as possible. Simply click the green button below to email the creator, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
